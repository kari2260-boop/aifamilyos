**第一节 单项填空（共10小题; 每小题1分, 满分10分）**

**从A、B、C三个选项中选择正确的答案填空。**

1\. As we know, Singapore is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ Asian country.

A. a B. an C. the

【答案】B

【解析】

【详解】句意：我们知道，新加坡是一个亚洲国家。

考查冠词辨析。此处表示一个亚洲国家，表泛指，且Asian是以元音音素开头的，故选B。

2\. The air in Huaihua is \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ than before.

A. good B. better C. best

【答案】B

【解析】

【详解】句意：怀化的空气比以前更好。

考查形容词比较级。good好的；better更好的，更好；best最好的，最。根据“than”可知，此处应用形容词比较级better。故选B。

3\. I want to buy something special for my grandmother, \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ her birthday is coming.

A. because B. although C. so

【答案】A

【解析】

【详解】句意：我想给我奶奶买点特别的东西，因为她的生日快到了。

考查连词。because因为；although虽然；so因此；空格前后句之间是因果关系，属于“前果后因”结构，应用because引导原因状语从句，故选A。

4\. The teacher told Jack \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ off the light when he left the classroom.

A. turn B. turning C. to turn

【答案】C

【解析】

【详解】句意：老师告诉杰克离开教室时把灯关掉。

考查非谓语。此处是tell sb. to do sth.“告诉某人做某事”的结构，空处用不定式作宾补，故选C。

5\. —Would you like to go to the history museum with me?

—\_\_\_\_\_\_\_.

A. Sure, I’d love to B. Yes, I do C. That’s right

【答案】A

【解析】

【详解】句意：——你愿意和我一起去历史博物馆吗？——当然，我想去。

考查情景交际。Sure, I’d love to当然，我想去；Yes, I do是的，我……；That’s right没错。根据“Would you like to go to the history museum with me”可知，对于对方的邀请表示同意或拒绝，故选A。

6\. Mingming will have a class meeting \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ 3:30 this afternoon.

A. on B. in C. at

【答案】C

【解析】

【详解】句意：今天下午三点半明明要开班会。

考查时间介词用法。in后接某年某月；on后接具体一天；at后接具体时刻。“3:30”是具体的时刻，故选C。

7\. We \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ each other since I came to Changsha, but we often send emails.

A. haven’t seen B. didn’t see C. don’t see

【答案】A

【解析】

【详解】句意：自从我来到长沙我们就没见过面，但我们经常发邮件。

考查现在完成时。根据“since I came to Changsha”可知，句子时态为现在完成时，其构成为：have/has+动词<img src="/Users/kari77/Desktop/中国K12知识库/真题库/中考真题/3 英语总复习/2015-2025年中考英语真题Markdown_可导入版/2.英语中考真题【2015-2024年】/2022中考英语真题143份11/精品解析：2022年湖南省怀化市中考英语真题（解析版）_media/media/image10.wmf" style="width:0.14583in;height:0.19444in" />过去分词，故选A。

8\. —Whose English book is this?

—It \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ be Lily’s, but I’m not sure.

A. might B. can’t C. must

【答案】A

【解析】

【详解】句意：——这是谁的英语书？——可能是莉莉的，但我不确定。

考查情态动词。might可能；can’t不可能；must一定；结合“but I’m not sure.”可知，此处是一种不确定的推测，应用might，故选A。

9\. Tea \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ by accident about 5000 years ago.

A. invented B. was invented C. is invented

【答案】B

【解析】

【详解】句意：茶是大约5000年前偶然发明的。

考查一般过去时态的被动语态。根据“5000 years ago”可知，此题时态为一般过去时，又因为“Tea”和动词“invent”之间是被动的关系，故此题应该用一般过去时态的被动语态，结构为was/were done，故选B。

10\. —Do you know \_\_\_\_\_\_\_?

—She is a teacher.

A. What is Linda’s job B. What Linda’s job is C. What was Linda’s job

【答案】B

【解析】

【详解】句意：——你知道琳达的工作是什么吗？——她是一名教师。

考查宾语从句。宾语从句要用陈述句语序，排除A/C选项，主句是一般现在时，从句时态不受限制，结合回答可知，询问现在的情况，应用一般现在时，故选B。

**第二节 完形填空（共10小题; 每小题1分, 满分10分）**

阅读下面的短文，从每题所给的A、B、C三个选项中选出可以填入空白处的最佳选项。

I often hear some students say English is difficult, so they can’t learn it well. <u>\_\_\_11\_\_\_</u> English is very easy for me. I’m good <u>\_\_\_12\_\_\_</u> it. I’m very glad to tell you <u>\_\_\_13\_\_\_</u> about how I study English.

<u>\_\_\_14\_\_\_</u>, I think an interest in English is very important. When I was in Grade Three, we had a new <u>\_\_\_15\_\_\_</u> — English, it was new for me. I was interested in it. I worked hard at it. Soon we had an English exam and I got a very good mark. How <u>\_\_\_16\_\_\_</u> I was! After that, I learned English harder. Our English teacher often teaches us English songs, and <u>\_\_\_17\_\_\_</u> sound nice. I often think how interesting English is!

Second, I think English is a foreign language. I learn it well in the following ways: <u>\_\_\_18\_\_\_</u> to the teacher carefully, read aloud and have a good vocabulary. Then practice again and again, never be tired. When I have a <u>\_\_\_19\_\_\_</u>, I often understand it by <u>\_\_\_\_\_20\_\_\_\_\_</u> our English teacher.

11\. A. And B. Or C. But

12\. A. in B. for C. at

13\. A. something B. anything C. nothing

14\. A. First B. Second C. Third

15\. A. class B. subject C. lesson

16\. A. sad B. sorry C. happy

17\. A. they B. it C. them

18\. A. hear B. listen C. tell

19\. A. idea B. suggestion C. question

20\. A. asking B. answering C. replying

【答案】11. C 12. C 13. A 14. A 15. B 16. C 17. A 18. B 19. C 20. A

【解析】

【导语】本文主要通过作者自己学习英语的经历介绍学好英语的方法。

【11题详解】

句意：但是对我而言英语却是很容易的。

And和；Or或者；But但是。根据上文“I often hear some students say English is difficult...”和下文“...English is very easy for me.”之间的逻辑关系可知，此处表示转折，应用but连接。故选C。

【12题详解】

句意：我擅长它。

in在……里；for为，为了；at在（某处）。根据句子结构可知，此处考查形容词短语be good at“擅长”。故选C。

【13题详解】

句意：我很乐意告诉你我是如何学好英语的。

something某事，某物；anything任何事物；nothing没有什么。something常用于肯定句，anything常用于疑问句和否定句。根据句子结构及“I’m very glad to tell you...”可知，此处表示肯定意义，应用something，指“某些东西”而不是“任何东西”。故选A。

【14题详解】

句意：首先，我认为对英语感兴趣很重要。

First首先，第一；Second第二；Third第三。根据句子结构可知，此处指first“首先”，表示叙述的顺序。故选A。

【15题详解】

句意：我读三年级的时候，我们有一个新的科目——英语。

class班级；subject科目；lesson课程。根据破折号后的“English”可知，此处指科目。故选B。

【16题详解】

句意：我感到多么的高兴啊！

sad难过；sorry对不起；happy高兴的。根据“Soon we had an English exam and I got a very good mark.”可知，此处指“我”因获得好成绩而感到高兴。故选C。

【17题详解】

句意：我们的英语老师经常教我们英文歌曲，而且他们听起来很动听。

they他们，她们，它们；it它；them他们，她们，它们。根据句子结构可知，此处缺少主语，应用人称代词主格they，指代上文中的“English songs”。故选A。

【18题详解】

句意：认真听老师……

hear听到；listen听；tell告诉。根据“to”和“the teacher”可知，此处考查动词短语listen to，to后接名词或代词作宾语。故选B。

【19题详解】

句意：当我遇到问题，我经常通过询问我们英语老师的办法来理解它。

idea主意；suggestion建议；question问题。根据动词“understand”可知，此处指问题。故选C。

【20题详解】

句意：当我遇到问题，我经常通过询问我们英语老师的办法来理解它。

asking问；answering问答；replying回复。根据句子结构可知，此处表示asking“询问”，后接“our English teacher”作宾语。故选A。

23\. What can visitors probably take to Lefeng Swimming Pool?

A. Swimming caps. B. Food. C. Rings.

【答案】21. B 22. C 23. A

【解析】

【导语】本文是Lefeng游泳池的广告，包括开放时间、票价、注意事项等。

【21题详解】

【答案】例文

It’s reported that labor education will be included in the education of middle schools and primary schools in China. I think it’s necessary. Because it is good for our health. We can relax from the hard study by doing labor.

I usually do labor at home such as doing the dishes, sweeping the floor and taking out the rubbish. At school I often clean the classroom and water flowers. I can not only keep healthy, but also develop good habits from it.

As for how to run the course in our school. I have some ideas. The school can offer different kinds of labor classes to teach us how to do the cleaning and grow plants. What’s more, the school can also hold competitions about the labor skills to see who is the best. Such activities make our school life more colorful and meaningful.

I am looking forward to taking the course in the new term. I believe I will do well in it.
